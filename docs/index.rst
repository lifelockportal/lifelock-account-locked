What Is a Locked LifeLock Account?
=================================

A locked LifeLock account occurs when access is temporarily restricted due to security reasons. This may happen after repeated incorrect login attempts, unusual account activity, or verification problems.

Account locking is a protective feature that helps prevent unauthorized users from accessing your personal information.

Why Your LifeLock Account May Be Locked
=======================================

Several situations can cause an account lock.

Common reasons include:

* Entering an incorrect password multiple times
* Forgetting login credentials
* Suspicious login attempts
* Signing in from an unfamiliar device
* Verification failures
* Outdated account information
* Security protection measures being triggered
* Temporary system restrictions

Identifying the reason behind the lock can help you choose the right solution.

Signs Your LifeLock Account Is Locked
====================================

You may notice the following issues when your account is locked:

* Unable to sign in successfully
* Login attempts are rejected
* Account access is temporarily restricted
* Security verification cannot be completed
* Error messages appear during login
* Password reset attempts do not immediately restore access

Recognizing these signs can help you determine whether your account requires unlocking.
